## Warehouse Management Systems (WMS)

Core systems for managing warehouse operations, inventory, and workflows.

- [Manhattan Associates WMS](https://www.manh.com/) — Enterprise warehouse management platform for complex operations.
- [SAP Extended Warehouse Management (EWM)](https://www.sap.com/products/scm/extended-warehouse-management.html) — Advanced WMS integrated with SAP supply chain solutions.
- [Oracle Warehouse Management](https://www.oracle.com/scm/logistics/warehouse-management/) — Cloud-based warehouse management platform.
- [Fishbowl Inventory](https://www.fishbowlinventory.com/) — Inventory and warehouse management solution for small to mid-sized businesses.

## Inventory Management

Tools for tracking stock levels, movements, and inventory optimization.

- [Zoho Inventory](https://www.zoho.com/inventory/) — Inventory management software for small and growing businesses.
- [inFlow Inventory](https://www.inflowinventory.com/) — Inventory tracking and order management platform.
- [TradeGecko (QuickBooks Commerce)](https://quickbooks.intuit.com/global/commerce/) — Inventory and order management platform.

## Automation & Robotics

Technologies enabling automated storage, picking, and warehouse operations.

- [AutoStore](https://www.autostoresystem.com/) — Automated storage and retrieval system (AS/RS) for warehouses.
- [Locus Robotics](https://locusrobotics.com/) — Autonomous mobile robots for warehouse picking and fulfillment.
- [GreyOrange](https://www.greyorange.com/) — Robotics and automation solutions for warehouses.

## Order Fulfillment & Logistics

Platforms for managing order processing, shipping, and fulfillment workflows.

- [ShipBob](https://www.shipbob.com/) — Fulfillment platform for e-commerce businesses.
- [ShipStation](https://www.shipstation.com/) — Shipping and order management platform.
- [Flexport](https://www.flexport.com/) — Logistics and supply chain platform with fulfillment capabilities.

## IoT & Tracking

Technologies for tracking assets, shipments, and warehouse conditions.

- [Zebra Technologies](https://www.zebra.com/) — RFID, barcode, and tracking solutions for warehouse operations.
- [Honeywell Warehouse Solutions](https://automation.honeywell.com/) — Automation and tracking technologies for warehouses.
- [Samsara](https://www.samsara.com/) — IoT platform for tracking assets and operations.

## Analytics & Optimization

Tools for improving warehouse efficiency, forecasting demand, and optimizing workflows.

- [Tableau](https://www.tableau.com/) — Data visualization platform used for warehouse analytics.
- [Power BI](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/) — Business analytics platform for reporting and insights.
- [Netstock](https://www.netstock.com/) — Inventory optimization and demand planning platform.
